TensorFlowJS Image Classifier

This is a simple image-classifier that runs in your browser using your web camera, a pre-trained machine-learning model from TensorFlowJS, and input from you.

Instructions

  1. Enable your webcam when prompted by your browser.
  2. Find three nearby things you want to use to train the image classifier. For example: your face, a book, a wristwatch.
  3. Decide which these three things you'll consider items A, B, and C.
  4. Hold A in front of your webcam and click the 'Add A' button repeatedly.
  5. Repeat for B (pressing the 'Add B' button) and for C (pressing the 'Add C' button).
  6. Your model will get better at predicting which of the three items captured by the webcam is showing as you train it. Those predictions will show below 'Predictions'.

Note: The classifier works best in Chrome.
